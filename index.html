Hello, my name is Wesley Foster. I was born in Texas and lived there for 22 years. After I graduated high school I went to college, I wasn't doing well, passed my classes but only barely. I was more concerend with playing video games than doing my schoolwork. When my technical training was completed I was sent to my first duty station in Washington (the state, not DC) and I LOVED IT there. I met my girlfriend within a few months of moving there on MySpace back when that site was still a thing. Together we adopted a Husky-mix named Halo who would become my precious baby girl for the next 12 years. I lived in Washington for a little over 3 years, then I was sent to Japan. The first 2 years were rough because I didn't care for some of the people I worked with, but eventually they left and I began to enjoy the remaining 2 years I spent there. I spent 4 years in Japan and had spent a total of 8 years in the Air Force but I just wasn't feeling it anymore so I decided to separate, and went back home to Texas. I enrolled in college, the same one I struggled at prior to joining the military, and finished the Computer Science degree I started some 10 years prior, this time making all A's in every class I took. After struggling to find a job I enjoyed, first at UPS which lasted all of 7 days, then at Fry's Electronics which lasted a little bit longer at a month, I rejoined the Air Force but in the Reserves, and at the same time I finally got a job I liked at Kohl's. I retrained into Fuels Management and did the Resesrve thing for 3 years until I transferred back to Acitve Duty, which was nearly 2 years ago. I am currently in California, but my goal is to eventaully make it back to Washington, becuase throughout all my moves my girlfriend has remained in WA, we've been on-and-off a few times, but we've currently been together since 2016.
